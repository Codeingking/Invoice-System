Privacy Policy

1. Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app only accesses your Google account with your permission to perform the tasks you authorize (such as uploading files to your own Google Drive).

2. Google OAuth Permissions

When you use this app:

  • You may be asked to sign in with your Google account.
  • The app requests permission to access your Google Drive (your account only).
  • This access is limited to file upload only and is not shared with anyone.

The app uses OAuth 2.0, a secure authorization protocol from Google. Your Google credentials are never stored or processed by us.

3. No Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. All actions like file uploads are performed directly between your browser and Google Drive via official APIs.

4. Data Security

We don’t store any user data, so there is no risk of data breach from our side.

5. Cookies

This app does not use cookies or any user-tracking mechanisms.

6. Third-Party Services

This app uses the Google Drive API and Google OAuth API. You can read more in Google’s Privacy Policy.
